3. What Data Gets Deleted

3.1 Immediately Deleted (Within 24 Hours)

  • Account access (login disabled)
  • Active user profile and preferences
  • Current session data and cached content
  • Marketing email subscriptions

3.2 Permanently Deleted (Within 30 Days)

  • All personal information (name, email, phone)
  • Business/salon information and branding
  • Generated content history and templates
  • Usage analytics linked to your account
  • Support tickets and communication history
  • Backup and archived data across all systems

3.3 Legal Retention (If Applicable)

  • Financial records may be retained for 7 years for tax compliance
  • Records are anonymized (no personal identifiers)
  • Required by law for business accounting purposes

11. Special Circumstances

11.1 Business Accounts

For salon/business accounts with multiple users:

  • Only the account owner can request full deletion
  • Individual users can request removal of their personal data
  • Business data may be retained if other users remain active

11.2 Legal Holds

In rare cases, deletion may be delayed if:

  • Data is subject to legal preservation requirements
  • Active investigation or legal proceeding requires data retention
  • We'll notify you immediately if this applies
